This computer comes with a pre-installed CAPTCHR Human Certificate Generator. You will be asked to submit your certificate to access CAPTCHR protected websites.

To claim a human certificate you will need to complete the following steps:

1. Visit the CAPTCHR website

2. Complete a series of CAPTCHRs

3. Collect the verification key

4. Use your key to unlock the CAPTCHR folder

5. Generate and download your CAPTCHR human certificate










You can use this certificate to access CAPTCHR protected websites.
