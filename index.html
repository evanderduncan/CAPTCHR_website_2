Welcome to CAPTCHR


A speculative Net.Art experience that uses the CAPTCHA interaction to explore the boundary between human and computer.

CAPTCHAs require humans to prove that they're not robots by passing tests designed to be impossible for computers.

As computers gain more human-like capabilities, what kind of data will be captured and measured to distinguish humans from computers.

CAPTCHR is a piece of intervention service design. Bringing awareness to the increasingly invasive and demeaning forms of human validation that we will be subjected to in the future.
